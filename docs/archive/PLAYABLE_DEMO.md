## ðŸŽ¯ How to Play

1. **Start the game**: `npm run play`

2. **Choose who to talk to**: Type 1, 2, or 3 to select an NPC

3. **Have a conversation**:
   - Option 1: Say something (freeform dialogue)
   - Option 2: Ask a question
   - Option 3: Say goodbye (ends conversation)

4. **Type anything you want**: The LLM will generate appropriate responses based on:
   - The character's personality
   - Their memories and backstory
   - Your relationship with them
   - The conversation history

5. **View stats**: Press 's' to see game statistics including:
   - Conversations had
   - Tokens generated
   - Relationships with NPCs
